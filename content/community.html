SproutCore has a vibrant community full of friendly folks willing to help. If you have questions, comments, suggestions or want to contribute, here are the best places to start:

  • SproutCore Google Group

    The SproutCore mailing list is the best place to reach everyone. It's generally low volume with high signal to noise ratio.

  • SproutCore Wiki

    Edited by the community, it has up-to-date links to programming guides and other resources.

  • #sproutcore on irc.freenode.net

    There are usually a few dozen people hanging out, including many of the members of the core team.

  • 3rd-Party Blogs

    Great resources for tutorials, news, and code.

  • SproutCore Tasks

    SproutCore's ticket system for tracking defects and feature requests. Signup to add your own.

    (By the way, Tasks is a SproutCore project. It's source code is available on GitHub.)

  • SproutCore Blog

    Major announcements, news, and assorted links.

  • SproutCore Reddit

    Contributed links about SproutCore.
